Meet the Creator

Jai is a young Comics Artist, Researcher, and forgotten archive enthusiast. Their work centers around their complex love of Baltimore's history and intersectional identities. Although they primarily work in comics they also explore Zines and short form writing as ways of expression. When they aren't hiding between library stacks they can be found spinning yarn for friends.

Jai first began their art career in Highschool, where they studied fine arts at their local magnet program. They discovered a love for afro futurism and expressions of self through art. Upon graduation they accepted a position at Just Lookin Art Gallery where they worked as a gallery assistant for three years. There they explored the power of print.
