The Baltimore Alternative Project

Why would you move to Baltimore? Isn't the crime insane? Have you seen the news out there? From the Headlines, you would think Baltimore is and has always been nothing but inner-city Mad Max. Let's challenge this. In Baltimore Alternative we follow our author Jai from their exploration of the controversial Drag Queen Divine to what it feels like when you suddenly find yourself on the other side of fear-mongering media coverage.
